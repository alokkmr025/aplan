Structure

Problem statement

Identification of the problem statement is one of the most important steps in building a solution to any social problem. In this round, you are expected to submit an overview of the exact problem you seek to solve during the Action Plan competition.

A-plan Submission

A-Plan submission round is the crux of Action plan. In this round, you are expected to submit a sound comprehensive solution. Also, the participants should have thought about the process of implementation of the solution taking guidelines from mentors.

Final presentation

Selected team has to present its solution to the eminent personalities.The participants are expected to present minimum viable product and customer validation of their solution.It is not necessary that participants actually implement the solution.

Mentorship

Mentors stand to gain the satisfaction of making a difference in fledgling entrepreneur's life and contributing back to the society. FAQ

  • Who can participate in Action Plan Competition?
  • Anyone, from students to working professionals across the nation, can participate. For more information refer Rules and Guidelines in Competition Manual.
  • I want to participate in A-Plan. What should I do?
  • Step 1- Register (link in the navigation bar) for A-Plan 2017.
    Step 2- You will have to provide Team leader's details, Team Members' details, postal and other details as required. You will receive a confirmation mail.
    Step 3- After getting confirmation mail, you can proceed for submission
  • What is the registration/participation fee for A-Plan?
  • There is NO registration/participation fee.
  • What is the last date of Registration?
  • The last date for registration is 20th October, 2017.
  • Can I submit additional documents along with the questionnaire?
  • Yes, you can submit extra document along with your questionnaire.
  • How can I confirm that I have submitted the questionnaire?
  • After submitting your entry, you will see a page confirming the receipt of your questionnaire along with your final submission.
  • Can I change my team later?
  • Yes, you can change your team info and other registration details by logging into the edit profile page. You can even add a team member or drop someone. However you cannot change the details of the Team Leader later.
    • Show More
  • What should be the size of my team?
  • Your team can consist of 3-8 members.
  • Can a team consist of people from different colleges?
  • Yes, teams can consist of individuals from different colleges.
  • Do I compulsorily need to have a team?
  • Yes, it's compulsory to have a team. It's very difficult for individuals to compete.
  • Does my Action plan have to be technology related?
  • No, we encourage all entries that can be developed into an Action plan. It must fit into model of Not for profit (NGO) or Social entrepreneurship.
  • How does the mentoring program work?
  • Mentors are experienced sector-specific experts who provide assistance and guidance to the participants. Mentors will offer suggestions and feedback to help participants develop and refine their Action plan. Participants are not required to have a mentor, although teams are strongly encouraged to take advantage of this opportunity. NOTE: - Mentors are there to guide you not to give you solutions of your problems.
